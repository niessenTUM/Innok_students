To disable the screen saver and password login on Ubuntu, you can follow these steps:

Disable Screen Saver:

Open the "Settings" application.

Click on "Power".

Set "Blank screen" to "Never".

Disable Password Login:

Open the "Settings" application.

Click on "Users".

Click on "Unlock" in the top right corner and enter your password.

Click on the "Automatic Login" switch to enable it.
